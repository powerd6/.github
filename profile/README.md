## Hi there 👋

powerd6 is a project focusing on creating interesting play by empowering combination and extension.

### :gear: How does it work?

> :construction_worker: Keep in mind, this is under construction, so not all things described exist (yet).

Players need an easy-to-reference material that unlocks their imagination during play.

By creating content in separate modules, and then combining them, players can choose what rules and worlds to use on their games.

A group might choose to play a classical fantasy scenario, with the extra rules built for horror games, building the framework for the somber tone they are trying to achieve.
